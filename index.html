WHAT IS ALL ABOUT TIME AND WORK??

In my understanding of this topic: In simple words, In a particular period of time, how much work can able to do by a particular person or a bunch of people.

IMPORTANT TO REMEMBER!!!

  • If A can do a job in "n" days, then A's 1 day's work will be 1/n.
  • If A's 1 day's is 1/n, then A can finish the work in n days.

Now will see a example...

Example:

If a man can do a piece of work in 8 days and a women can do the same work in 5 days, in how many days will both of them together complete it?

SOLUTION...

Man's one day work = 1/8 and women's one day work will be 1/5
then (man +women)'s one day will be (1/8+1/5) = (5+8)/40 = 13/40
Note: In the above step , I just took L.C.M for the numbers.
so They both together complete the work in 40/13 that is 3 1/13 days..
